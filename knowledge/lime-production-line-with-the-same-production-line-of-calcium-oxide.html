Lime production line with the same production line of calcium oxide?

Time: 2015-02-04

Quicklime and slaked lime is divided into two kinds, lime is the main component of calcium oxide (CaO), it is also known as calcium oxide, lime appearance is a white solid, add lime to absorb moisture or water becomes slaked lime, hydrated lime also known as calcium hydroxide Ca ( OH) 2, "lime." These two different uses are widely lime, slaked lime slurry formulated into, lime plaster, lime mortar, etc., may be used as coating materials and tile adhesives. Calcium oxide production line is one of lime production line.
