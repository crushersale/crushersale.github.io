The common problems of sand making machine industry

Time: 2015-01-04

Sand making machine as the preferred equipment for artificial sand and widely used for crushing various industry. Shanghai zenith of independent research and development of sand making machine can be widely used in stone, cement and various metallurgical slag industry in the soft, medium hard material crushing, plastic, also suitable for high quality stone production mechanism of sand, highway, railway, water conservancy, airport, construction and other industries.

Users are most concerned about the use of sand making machine is when sand making machine production and sand making machine which problems occur in the process of use. Below we introduce the common problems under the sand making machine in the production process and solving methods for user reference.

1. product size too large. This is mainly because the sand making machine belt is too loose to cause, just need to adjust the belt tension bolt can be, but it is easy to ignore in the production process problems.
2. sand making machine body swing. The cause of sand body swing is feed grain is too large, then the increase of sand making machine load, resulting in regulating feed size, impeller channel blocking material, only need to remove the channel plugging, wear-resisting block grinding caused stress, replacing the full set of the wear-resistant body.
3. for sand production metal impact sound. This is due to the impeller and the collision of the distributor, distributor installation position can be adjusted.
