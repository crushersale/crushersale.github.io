HP series hydraulic cone crusher performance superiority

Time: 2015-02-04

With the development of hydraulic technology, achieved through the hydraulic adjusting the discharge cone crusher, known as hydraulic cone crusher, the crusher has the advantages of simple structure, low manufacturing cost, high production capacity, convenient maintenance, hydraulic adjustment and overload protection performance, especially easy to realize the automatic control.

The hydraulic cone crusher is of two kinds: single cylinder hydraulic cone crusher and multi cylinder hydraulic cone crusher. Hydraulic cone crusher made by laminating crushing principle, also can realize the selective crushing and to a certain extent, to meet the "more crushing and less grinding" process requirements. Especially the HP series hydraulic cone crusher performance is more superior, below we find out in detail.

1, the operation is convenient

HP series hydraulic cone crusher hydraulic device mainly used for adjusting the breaker gape size, advanced hydraulic cone crusher and the traditional spring cone crusher contrast, the process of adjustment need not operation in the entire row ore mouth, just press the button to complete the adjustment process, not only can improve the work efficiency, reduces the labor intensity of workers.

2, liner replacement convenient

All the parts of HP series hydraulic cone crusher can directly from the top or side of the disassembly and maintenance, dynamic cone and the fixed cone assembly and disassembly is more convenient, without dismantling the machine frame, a fastening bolt, liner wear needs replacement more convenient.

3, the lubrication system is advanced in design?

HP series hydraulic cone crusher adopts high performance non-contact labyrinth seals, basically realizes zero pollution of dust. Equipped with hydraulic and lubricating system and automatic intelligent control system, reduces the time cost of lubrication, lubrication work easier, and the wear volume reduced equipment parts.

4, high production efficiency

Machine will be combined with the speed and stroke higher HP series hydraulic cone crusher, improving the power ratings and bearing capacity, but also improve the crushing ratio and production efficiency of equipment.

5, many kinds of crushing cavity

HP series hydraulic cone crusher crushing cavity multiple machine diversity, flexible application and adaptability, from the standard super thick cavity type to the short head of ultrafine cavity can transform to meet the requirements of production.
