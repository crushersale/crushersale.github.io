PF impact crusher adopts the latest technology

Impact crusher can be used to crush large, medium and small size materials (granite, limestone, concrete, etc) with max feed size less than 500mm and crushing strength less than 360Mpa. Impact crusher for the quarry is widely applied for crushing ore in mining, highway, railway, construction, etc. Presently, by practices, impact crusher for the quarry's comprehensive function is superior to the cone crusher and the hammer crusher.

Impact Crusher adopts the newest manufacturing technology and unique structure design, its crushed product is with perfect cubic shape, no tension and crack. This crusher can crush materials with size smaller than 500mm and resisting strength less than 350MPa, such as granite, limestone, and concrete aggregate, etc. It is widely used in road construction, railway and other industries.

Structure and Working Principle of Stone Impact Crusher

Stone impact crusher is composed of chassis, rotor and impact plate, which is of simple structure. In operating process of impact crushing machine, plate hammer and rotor rotate in high speed and impact with materials; in the repeated impacting process, materials will collide with each other. Due to collision between materials and impact of plate hammer and impact plate, materials generates fissure and dehiscence until they are being crushed. When large materials and materials which are hard to crush are sandwiched between rotor and impact plate, impact plate is suffered from high pressure, thus making impact force backward so as to make materials which are hard to crush go through and prevent rotor from being damaged.

impact crusher machine Application:

This series of product is usually applied in the secondary crushing, and the compressive strength is up to 350MPa. The impact crusher is applicable for crushing limestone, concrete, basalt, and especially applicable for the preparation of road stone. It is widely used in various fields of mining, smelting, building materials, highway, railway, water conservancy and chemical industry, etc.

This series of product is a Three cavity crusher with three-level impact plates. The impact plate of rear chamber plays a role in reeling. The impact crusher machine can handle materials with a side length below 100-800mm, and it is applicable for crushing medium hard materials. The crusher's compressive strength is up to 350MPa, the discharge particle size is relatively small, the material particle size is good, and particles are shown in a cubic shape. The rotor components are elaborated, the dynamic and static balance of rotor is good, and the machine can be in smooth operation with low noise.
